Project 1: Used Car Pricing Research

Project Context

This project, completed for my Marketing Research course, was aimed at understanding what factors influence the prices of used vehicles. Particularly, analyzing what impact mileage, vehicle age, make, and model have on used car listing prices.

Research Question

Research Question:

How do mileage, age, make, model, and brand reputation influence used car pricing?

Data and Method

  • Data source: CarMax, Carvana
  • Sample size: 60 vehicle listings
  • Main variables: Mileage, vehicle age, make, model
  • Analysis method: Regression
  • Purpose of analysis: Determining what factors influence the price of used vehicles, in order to help used car buyers evaluate whether they are being fairly charged.

Skills Demonstrated

  • Market research
  • Data collection
  • Regression analysis
  • Business interpretation
  • Written communication

What I Learned

This project taught me that pricing decisions are influenced by vehicle mileage as well as make. Mileage being directly measurable, while make carried a more perceptual variable weight. Brand reputation has a signficant influence on the percieved value of a vehicle.

Skills Demonstrated
